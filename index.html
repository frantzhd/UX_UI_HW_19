Hello there, I’m Frantz Drouillard!
I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design. I have translated my visual communication problem solving skills into solving the problems of user experiences. I’ve worn a lot of hats  over the years from design drop shipping to production management and that’s due to my flexibility and adaptability. I’m a team player who’s always willing, ready, and able to learn more.

My UX Skills

User Research

When conducting user research I think of a question relating to the subject matter. Like , “What are a users motivations for why they engage with something or continue with a particular behavior  

User Empathy

When it comes to user empathy I like to think of what is the best interaction/experience they can have. I put myself in their shoes and wonder what difficulties they can have that would lead to the worst experience they can have then I think of what can be improved to hopefully avoid the bad experience altogether

Qualitative Interviews

Quantitative information is important but I really like qualitative information because of the deeper understanding and connection it gives us to people who are more than numbers. Like I mentioned before i think of what motivates people in their actions and behavior and that shapes the questions I create to better understand their thinking process which helps me to better understand them.
